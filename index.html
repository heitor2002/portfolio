    First contact with programming

    At the age of 13 I started my journey in programming, initially programming lego robots using Lego NXT. During high school the robots began to be programmed using Arduino, where I had the first contact with the C++ language. At the end of high school, I was interested in front-end web development, because in it I saw the opportunity to solve problems, boost my creative power and level up every day.

    Heitor Montagnini

    Brazilian born in a small town of just over 20,000 inhabitants, My name is Heitor Montagnini and I'll tell you a little about me. I am 20 years old and since I was a child I am fascinated by the creative power of the human being; in childhood I wrote short stories and was passionate about drawings, which made me arouse an even greater curiosity for the design of things.

    On weekends I visited gaming sites that served as an opening for my interest in web design, however, I had no instruction to dive into this programming universe. It was when at school I discovered robotics, which allowed me to learn a little more about logic as a method of solving problems, since it was necessary to build small ones for lego and program that would solve the challenges.

    In high school I finally had access to a programming language, this time C++, a language used in Arduino. From this, I decided to invest in the web and delve into front-end development. I worked as an administrative assistant in a health company and started using this money as an investment for my future, and today I seek to know something new every day.
